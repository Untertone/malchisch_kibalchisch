# VR Troubleshooting


Modern VR development spans a wide range of headsets, each with its own hardware specifications, software layers, operating systems, and unique behaviors. While the Engine is built to support most major VR devices out of the box, differences between platforms can occasionally result in unexpected behavior or compatibility issues.


This series of articles provides a collection of known issues, workarounds, and best practices to help you diagnose and resolve common VR-related problems.
